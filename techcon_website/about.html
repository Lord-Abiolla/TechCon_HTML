History

TechCon began as a small regional meetup in 2014, organized by a passionate group of developers and tech entrepreneurs, with an aim to create space for tech talent to network, learn, and showcase innovation. Initially attended by just over 150 participants, the event grew in size and influence, moving to different cities each year and attracting wider participation from tech hubs across various continents. By 2020, TechCon had evolved into a full-scale conference, featuring workshops, product launches, and panel discussions with leading tech figures.

Mission

Our mission is to expand beyond networking to include advocacy for digital inclusion, education, and homegrown solutions
